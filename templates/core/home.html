Why businesses use MintKit Hub

Built for real-world services and products

Hair & beauty, gyms, trainers, tutors, local events, cafés, makers, small shops… Anyone who sells time, sessions, experiences or physical products and wants modern digital cards on top: gift cards, passes, memberships, tickets and vouchers.

Cards that behave like passes

Each digital card has a clear status (unused, redeemed, expired) and a QR code that can be scanned at the door, at the till, or wherever you validate access.

Lightweight, not a full shop system

You don't need a huge e-commerce platform. MintKit Hub focuses on one thing: selling and managing digital access in a clean way. Your existing website, socials or offline marketing can simply link to your MintKit storefront.

What the flow looks like

1. Set up your profile

Create an account, add your business name, contact details and logo. Your dashboard becomes the “control room” for everything else.

2. Publish your storefront

Add a short headline and description, decide if it's public, and share the link with your customers. No full website rebuild needed.

3. Create and manage digital cards

Use MintKit Studio to design the actual gift cards, tickets and vouchers. Customers buy them online; you scan and manage them from your own dashboard.
