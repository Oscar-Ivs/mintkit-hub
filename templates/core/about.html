About MintKit Hub

MintKit Hub is your control centre for creating and managing digital gift cards, tickets and vouchers. It connects a familiar Web 2 experience (this dashboard) with Web 3 technology running on the blockchain.

Two parts, one platform

MintKit Hub (this site)

  • Sign up and manage your business profile.
  • Create and edit your public storefront page.
  • Configure which digital cards you want to offer.
  • Connect payments via Stripe.
  • View basic stats and manage your subscription.

MintKit Studio (on-chain Minting Studio)

  • Secure minting of your digital cards as NFTs/tokens.
  • Asset storage on the Internet Computer blockchain.
  • Admin tools for testing, previewing and managing cards.
  • Future features such as advanced analytics and VetKeys-style offline redemption.

Who is MintKit for?

MintKit is designed for small businesses and creators who want to offer modern digital cards without building their own Web 3 stack:

  • Hairdressers, beauty salons and barbers.
  • Caf√©s, restaurants and food trucks.
  • Gyms, sports clubs and community centres.
  • Event organisers and ticketed experiences.
  • Independent creators who sell access passes or digital rewards.

How it works in practice

  1. You create a business profile and storefront here in MintKit Hub.
  2. You define one or more digital cards (gift cards, tickets or vouchers).
  3. Your customers buy cards via your storefront (Stripe payments).
  4. Each card is linked to a secure asset minted via MintKit Studio.
  5. The customer receives an email with a direct link to digital card & dynamic QR code.

For the college project, some parts are implemented as working stubs (for example Stripe and advanced analytics). The core flows - business profile, storefront management and the bridge to MintKit Studio - are the focus of this version.
