About MintKit Hub

MintKit Hub is the control centre for creating and managing digital gift cards, tickets and vouchers. It connects a familiar Web 2 dashboard experience (this site) with Web 3 technology running on blockchain infrastructure.

Two parts, one platform

MintKit Hub (this site)

  • Sign up and manage a business profile.
  • Create and edit a public storefront page.
  • Choose which digital cards to feature on the storefront.
  • Connect payments (Stripe integration).
  • View basic stats and manage a subscription.

MintKit Studio (on-chain minting studio)

  • Secure minting of digital cards as NFTs/tokens.
  • Asset storage on dedicated blockchain infrastructure.
  • Admin tools for testing, previewing and managing cards.
  • Future options for analytics and offline redemption flows.

Who is MintKit for?

MintKit is designed for small businesses and creators who want to offer modern digital cards without building their own Web 3 stack:

  • Hairdressers, beauty salons and barbers.
  • Caf√©s, restaurants and food trucks.
  • Gyms, sports clubs and community centres.
  • Event organisers and ticketed experiences.
  • Independent creators who sell access passes or digital rewards.

How it works in practice

  1. Create a business profile and storefront in MintKit Hub.
  2. Add one or more featured digital cards (gift cards, tickets, vouchers).
  3. Share the public storefront link with customers.
  4. Customers buy through the storefront (payments), and cards are issued and managed through the platform.
