Quick Start (Steps A-Z)
1 Explore MintKit
  • Browse Explore without signing up.
  • See what others have built.
2 Register / Login
  • Register for a free account.
  • Already have one? Sign in.
3 Create your Storefront
  • Go to My storefront and fill in business details.
  • Click Save storefront.
4 Style your Storefront
  • Best on laptop/desktop or tablet.
  • Open Edit layout in the preview.
  • Move/resize/hide blocks ‚Üí click Save layout.
5 Share your Storefront
  • Use copy/share buttons in My storefront.
  • To appear in Explore: enable Make my storefront public ‚Üí Save.
6 Start Trial
  • From Pricing or Dashboard, click Start trial.
7 Open Studio
  • Open Studio.
  • Sign in with Internet Identity (no password).
8 Mint Cards in Studio
  • Create a Style.
  • Create a Collection (max 50 recommended).
  • Mint in the Mint tab.
  • Manage in Manager.
9 Send or Copy a Card
  • Use Send by Email in Manager.
  • Or use Copy for MintKit Hub.
10 Link Cards to your Storefront
  • Go to My storefront ‚Üí Featured digital cards.
  • Click Paste from Studio ‚Üí add description + buy link.
11 Add a Buy Link (Stripe)
  • Create a Stripe Payment Link and paste it into the card.
