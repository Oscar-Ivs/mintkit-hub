Quick Start (Steps A-Z)
1) Explore MintKit
Explore MintKit Hub without signing up.
Go to Explore to see public storefronts.
2) Register / Login
Create a free account to build your storefront. Email is required for sending card links.
Register Â· Login
3) Create your Storefront
In My Storefront fill in:
  • Business Category & Region
  • Logo (optional), headline, description
  • Contact details
  • Click Save storefront
My Storefront
4) Style your Storefront (Layout Editor)
Best on laptop/desktop or tablet.
  • Open Edit layout in the preview
  • Move / resize slightly / hide blocks
  • Use Fit to preview overall page
  • Click Save layout
5) Share your Storefront
Use the Copy/Share buttons in My Storefront.
  • To appear in Explore, enable Make my storefront public
  • Click Save storefront
6) Start Trial
Start a 14-day trial from Dashboard or Pricing to unlock Studio tools.
Pricing Â· Dashboard
7) Open Studio
Studio lets you create and manage gift cards, tickets, and vouchers, send by email, and redeem via QR/manual code.
Open Studio
8) Mint Cards in Studio
  • Style Editor: create a card style
  • Collections: create a collection (max 50)
  • Mint: mint the amount you need (you can mint more later)
  • Manager: view cards, statuses, QR codes
9) Send or Copy a Card
In Studio Manager, click Send by Email to deliver a viewer link to the recipient. For your storefront, click Copy for MintKit Hub.
